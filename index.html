RESEARCH PAPERS

Optimal Key Consensus in Presence of Noise

Zhengzhong Jin, Yunlei Zhao

In this work, we discovered an efficiency upper bound for the reconciliation mechanism of LWE/RLWE-based key exchange, which helps two communicating parties reaching consensus from close values obtained by exchanging their LWE/RLWE samples. Then we proposed a general reconciliation mechanism. The generality allowed us to take balanced among parameters, and resulted in a more efficient LWE-based key exchange scheme.

Submitted to IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy 2017, under review.

Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/1611.06150 (Will be updated soon)

This research is under the guidance of Prof. Yunlei Zhao.

Supported by the Key Program of the National Natural Science Foundation of China: research on lattice-based quantum-resist public key cryptography theory and technology, U1536205.
